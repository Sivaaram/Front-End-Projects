ABOUT US

Welcome to our Grape Garden, where we grow and sell the finest grapes from the renowned grape paradise of Nashik. With decades of expertise in viticulture, our team ensures that every grape we cultivate meets the highest standards of quality and taste.Nestled in Nashik, famously known as the "Paradise of Earth" for its temperate climate and fertile loamy soil, our vineyard benefits from ideal conditions for grape cultivation. The region's rich organic soil, coupled with excellent drainage and aeration, creates a perfect environment for our grapevines to thrive.

VARIETIES

Concord

Concord grapes are used to make pie, jam, juice and to flavored candy. Concord grapes are also often used to make Kosher wine.Concords are in season, from September through late October.

Moon Drop

The long, dark purple-blue grapes that look a little like a finger are called Moon Drop, or Witch's Finger.

Canadice

Canadice is a cultivar of seedless red grape with a bit of a spicy flavor. It is a late season cultivar ripening about mid-September into October

Niagara

The flavor of the Niagara grape is jammy, with floral notes and a lemon rind twist. It's sometimes used for wine, but mainly this variety is turned in to jelly or juice.

Cotton Candy

The Cotton Candy is a juicy, sweet, candy-like and limited-time grape.Cotton Candy grapes are cross-bred naturally on the vines.

Crimson Seedless

Crimson grapes are the most common and popular type of red grapes. Their colours range from dark pink to red and they offer an extremely sweet and juicy experience.
